## Why we link instead of hosting

The ECG Matrix is an open-source tool, but the PDFs are copyrighted by ECOnGOOD.
We link to the official source to ensure you always get the latest version and to respect the work of the ECOnGOOD community.

## About the Matrix

The ECG Matrix consists of:
- **5 value columns:** Human Dignity · Solidarity · Ecology · Transparency · Meaning
- **5 stakeholder groups:** Suppliers · Finance · Employees · Customers · Society
- **= 20 CommonGood themes** that describe an organization's contribution to the CommonGood

Maximum score: **1,000 CommonGood points**
Negative score for harmful practices: up to **−3,600 points**
